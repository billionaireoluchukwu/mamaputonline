Our Story

Mama's Ofe Akwu was breathtaking.

From the akwu she grew organically in her backyard, to her choice of food ingredients and her secret blend of spices handed over to her by her mothers, Mama's food was a banger, back to back!

Every pot she cooked was a labour of love, every meal she served was a celebration of Nigerian culture and culinary heritage. Every cleared plate was a testament to her skill, passion, and dedication to the art of cooking.

That same spirit lives in every pot at MamaPutOnline.
