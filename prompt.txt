# 1. Initial Website Concept Prompt

> Build a Nigerian food ordering landing page called **MamaPutOnline**.
> It should represent Nigerian food culture, home cooking, and the feeling of eating food made by Mama.
> The brand should feel warm, traditional, premium, and nostalgic.

> “Nigeria On A Plate”

* Busy professionals
* Students
* Families
* Nigerians in urban areas
